About me

I'm Aiya After completing my studies in UX/UI design at Cornerstone International College in Vancouver, BC, I gained hands-on industry experience through a completed internship. I am now continuing to expand my skills by studying Web Development at Tamwood Careers.

My design journey began in Thailand, inspired by a family that nurtured my creativity from a young age. I loved painting, drawing, and making music as a kid, which shaped me into an enthusiastic person who enjoys various aspects of design and continually explores new opportunities.

After graduating from the College of Music, I worked as a sound engineer in movie production while running my own clothing store, handling everything from branding and graphics to customer interaction.

During that time, I discovered my passion for engaging with customers, gathering feedback, and designing solutions for their needs. That’s when I realized – becoming a UX/UI designer was the perfect path for me.
